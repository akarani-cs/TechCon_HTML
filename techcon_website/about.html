Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup of software developers passionate about open-source technologies. Over the years, it has grown into a global conference attracting thousands of tech enthusiasts, innovators, and industry leaders.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to empower the tech community through shared knowledge, collaboration, and inspiration. We aim to spotlight breakthrough ideas and foster innovation across all areas of technology.
